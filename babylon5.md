# Babylon 5 Collectible Card Game

## Overview

[Boardgame Geek](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/2640/babylon-5-collectible-card-game)

## Specifications

* Deck Size: At least 45
* Duplicate Card Limit: 3
* Players: 1 - 4
* Card Types: 10
* Factions/Teams: 4+
